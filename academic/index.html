Important Examination Instructions

Ensure strict compliance with the following guidelines during the End-Autumn Examinations:

  • Entry Time: Candidates must report to the examination hall 20 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Admit Card & ID: Bring a physical copy of the ERP-generated Admit Card and official IIT KGP student identity card. No entry without both.
  • Prohibited Items: Smartwatches, mobile phones, programmable calculators, tablets, and any electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Stationery: Candidates must bring their own pens, pencils, rulers, and non-programmable calculators. Lending of materials is not allowed.

Complete Academic Examination Code of Conduct

This document details the complete academic conduct and disciplinary policy for examination periods:

  1. Academic Malpractice: Any student found utilizing unauthorized scripts, writing on desks, or communicating with other candidates will be immediately deregistered from the course and referred to the Senate Standing Committee on Disciplinary Action.
  2. Answer Script Guidelines: Fill in all header information on the main script and supplementary sheets accurately. Loose sheets must be tied securely.
  3. Medical Absences: In case of acute medical emergencies, the student must report to BCR Technology Hospital and submit a medical certificate verified by the PMO within 48 hours to apply for a 'De-novo' exam request.
  4. Grading & Moderation: Raw marks are moderated by the Departmental Academic Committee before grade allocation on the portal.

CDC Placement Rules & Regulations

All registered students must strictly follow the CDC rules. Violations will lead to immediate debarment:

  • One Student One Job Policy: Once you are selected by a company, you are immediately debarred from applying to any other placement processes. Exception: Dream/Core upgrade option.
  • Attendance Policy: 100% attendance is mandatory for pre-placement talks (PPT) and online/offline tests. Absenteeism without prior HMC/CDC permission will result in debarment.
  • Information Verification: Any discrepancy in CGPA, credits, or resumes submitted will result in immediate suspension from placements for the academic year.
  • Interview Etiquette: Candidates must report in official business attire. Non-attendance of a scheduled interview slot will attract immediate disciplinary action.
